Fried eggs recipe

  1. Get pan, eggs, salt and butter or oil
  2. Heat the pan. Start by putting your pan on the burner for 1 minute
  3. Add butter or oil
  4. Crack eggs into pan and add salt. Fry for 5 minutes
  5. Your eggs are ready!
